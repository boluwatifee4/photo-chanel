ABOUT ME

I'm a photographer based in Lagos State, Nigeria who specializes in photographing portraits, weddings, conferences, seminars, picture framing; I also have a mild airplane obsession. I grew up in Ibadan Oyo state: the largest city in Africa. Here I was lucky enough to meet a number of amazing teachers who opened my eyes to the world of Art in photography and design; this would alter the path of my life in more ways than I could ever imagine. After studying Business Administration at Lagos state polytechnic. After a couple of years and too much planning, I found myself taking up an offer to photograph a few homes for a client I'd met while moving. What started by chance turned out to be the perfect mix of technical challenge and creative outlet, and I decided right there and then that it absolutely must be my career. In 2010, I founded the Photography outfit a resource for An event and portrait taking photographers and those people in the industry seeking to learn about the framing, lighting, editing, shooting, and theory of aspects of photography.
